The problem

Sound familiar?

Ten different apps

Tasks here, shopping there, calendar somewhere else. Nothing talks to anything.

Subscriptions, forever

Notion, Google One, iCloud — another invoice every year. No way out.

Your data, everywhere

Your calendar on Google's servers, your notes on Dropbox. Who actually owns your family's data?

There's a calmer way. Self-hosted, and yours.

Why Yuvomi

Built for families, not for profit.

No subscriptions, no vendor lock-in, no data leaving your home. Just the tools your household actually uses.

Privacy first

Optional AES-256 database encryption via SQLCipher (enabled in the recommended Docker setup). Zero telemetry. Your data never leaves your server.

Fully self-hosted

Runs on a Raspberry Pi, a NAS, or any server. Docker or Podman makes setup a one-liner.

Open source

MIT licensed. Inspect, modify, extend, contribute. Built in the open for families who value transparency.

Zero build step

Pure ES modules, vanilla JS, plain CSS. No bundler, no transpiler, no framework. It ships what we write.

Features

Everything your household needs

A complete set of tools for families — designed to work together, not in fourteen separate tabs.

Tasks

Everyone knows what needs doing.

Shared tasks with deadlines, priorities, subtasks and recurring schedules — assign to several family members at once.

  • Kanban board with one-tap status changes
  • Multi-member assignment with stacked avatars
  • Optional read-only Apple Reminders import
Calendar

One calendar for the whole family.

Google Calendar, iCloud, Nextcloud — all in one place. Two-way CalDAV sync, public ICS subscriptions, and built-in public & school holidays.

  • Two-way Google & CalDAV sync
  • Recurring events with file attachments
  • Readable month & agenda views
Meals & recipes

Dinner sorted before anyone asks.

A weekly drag-and-drop planner with ingredient lists that export straight to the shopping list in one click.

  • Drag-and-drop weekly planner
  • Reusable recipes you can scale & duplicate
  • One-tap export to your shopping list
Budget

No surprise at the end of the month.

Track who spent what, split shared costs automatically, and see exactly where the money goes.

  • 23 currencies, recurring entries, loan tracking
  • Split expenses with automatic debt simplification
  • Subscription tracker with renewals, budgets & alerts
Shopping

The list everyone actually checks.

One shared shopping list for the whole family — organized by aisle, updating in real time.

  • Aisle categories & swipe gestures
  • Add a whole meal plan in one tap
  • Touch-safe quick add on mobile

And there's more

One app, fourteen modules

Each module is independent. Use what fits your family, skip what doesn't.

Documents

Family files with previews, visibility controls and storage badges. New uploads and calendar attachments can use WebDAV; Paperless-ngx and Papra DMS integration is optional.

Notes

Colored sticky notes with Markdown. Perfect for recipes, family memos and quick reminders.

Contacts

Shared family directory with CardDAV sync, vCard import/export and multi-account support.

Birthdays

Birthday tracker with automatic annual calendar events, age display and custom reminders.

Housekeeping

Manage household staff — schedules, check-in/out, daily or hourly billing, chores and supplies.

Recipes

Create, duplicate and scale reusable recipes. Pre-fill meal slots or save any meal as a recipe.

Reminders

Time-based notifications on tasks and calendar events, with an in-app badge so nothing slips, plus opt-in push notifications that reach you even when the app is closed.

Backup & SSO

Scheduled backups with pre-restore rollback, plus optional single sign-on via any OIDC provider.

Every screen, carefully crafted
